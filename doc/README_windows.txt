Intro
-----
Renesis is a free open source project derived from Bitcoin, with the goal of providing
a long-term energy-efficient Rensis based crypto-currency. Built on the foundations of
Bitcoin, PPCoin and Blackcoin, innovations such as LTME , proof-of-stake and the algo
help further advance the field of crypto-currency.
